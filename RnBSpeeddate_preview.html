Good to Know

Frequently asked

What's the minimum age?

Chemistry Lab is a 29+ event. ID may be checked at the door — please bring a valid ID.

How does the speed dating work?

Each date lasts about 2.5 minutes. In the first round the gentlemen stay seated and the ladies rotate. In the second round it switches: the ladies stay seated and the gentlemen rotate. After every date you'll have a moment to mark on a (digital) form / app how you felt about the date. Only mutual matches receive each other's details — within 72 hours after the night.

What is the dress code?

Dress to impress. Classy, confident, effortless. No sneakers, no hoodies. Think: what would you wear to impress your date on the very first night? One man and one woman will be crowned for showing up the flyest.

What kind of music will be played?

R&B, Hip Hop and Slow Jams from 90's classics to modern favorites. After the speed dating rounds the night closes with an R&B party.

I'm coming alone — will I feel welcome?

Absolutely — most of our guests come solo. Our hosts walk the floor all night to make sure no one feels alone. They'll introduce you, break the ice, and help the connections flow.

Will there be photos or video taken?

A photographer and videographer will be present for marketing purposes. Prefer to stay off camera? Just let the crew know — we got you.

How do I get there?

The event is at Third Place Rotterdam. Full address details will be sent to ticket holders before the event.

When do tickets go on sale?

Tickets will be available soon. Follow us on Instagram or check back here — we'll announce the drop and limited tickets will sell fast.
